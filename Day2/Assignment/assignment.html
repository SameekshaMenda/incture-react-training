Google's algorithm prioritizes "people-first content," meaning content that is genuinely helpful, informative, and trustworthy for users. To rank well, articles should be well-structured, easy to read, and offer unique insights. Google also considers factors like expertise, authority, and trustworthiness (E-A-T) when evaluating content.
